# What is Developing Workflow Diagrams?

Developing workflow diagrams involves creating visual representations of the processes and interactions within the system. These diagrams help in understanding and optimizing workflows.

## Key Objectives:
1. Visualize the sequence of tasks and interactions.
2. Identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies in workflows.
3. Communicate system design to stakeholders.

Workflow diagrams are essential for planning and optimizing the JDHP publishing house's operations.

# What is Survey Agent?

The Survey Agent is responsible for conducting surveys and gathering feedback from the target audience. It ensures that the JDHP publishing house understands audience needs and preferences.

## Key Features:
1. **Survey Creation**: Design and distribute surveys to the audience.
2. **Data Collection**: Gather responses and analyze feedback.
3. **Insights Generation**: Provide actionable insights based on survey data.

The Survey Agent is essential for maintaining a customer-centric approach in the JDHP publishing house.

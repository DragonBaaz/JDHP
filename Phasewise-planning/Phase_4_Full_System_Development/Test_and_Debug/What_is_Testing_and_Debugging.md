# What is Testing and Debugging?

Testing and debugging involve validating the system's functionality and resolving any issues that arise. This ensures that the JDHP publishing house operates reliably and efficiently.

## Key Objectives:
1. Identify and fix errors in the system.
2. Validate the functionality of all agents and workflows.
3. Optimize system performance and reliability.

Testing and debugging are critical for ensuring the success of the JDHP publishing house.

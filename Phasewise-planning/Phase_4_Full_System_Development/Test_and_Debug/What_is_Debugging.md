# What is Debugging?

Debugging is the process of identifying and resolving errors or issues in a system. It ensures that the JDHP publishing house operates smoothly and without interruptions.

## Key Features:
1. **Error Detection**: Identify issues in the system.
2. **Root Cause Analysis**: Determine the underlying cause of errors.
3. **Issue Resolution**: Implement fixes to resolve problems.

Debugging is essential for maintaining the reliability of the JDHP publishing house.

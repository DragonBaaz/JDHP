# What is Report Quality?

Report quality refers to the accuracy, relevance, and presentation of the research reports produced by the JDHP system. High-quality reports are essential for building trust and credibility with the target audience.

## Key Factors:
1. **Accuracy**: Ensuring the information is correct and well-researched.
2. **Relevance**: Addressing the needs and interests of the target audience.
3. **Presentation**: Delivering reports in a professional and visually appealing format.

Maintaining high report quality is crucial for the success of JDHP.

# What is Sales Performance?

Sales performance measures the revenue and sales generated by the research reports. It is a key indicator of the market success and financial viability of the JDHP publishing house.

## Key Metrics:
1. **Revenue**: Total income generated from report sales.
2. **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of visitors who purchase reports.
3. **Customer Retention**: Number of repeat buyers.

Tracking sales performance helps JDHP identify successful strategies and areas for improvement.

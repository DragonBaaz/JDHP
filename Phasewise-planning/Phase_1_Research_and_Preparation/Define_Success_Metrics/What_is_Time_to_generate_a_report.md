# What is Time to Generate a Report?

Time to generate a report refers to the duration required to complete the entire lifecycle of a research report, from topic selection to publishing. This metric is critical for evaluating the efficiency of the system.

## Key Factors:
1. **Automation**: The degree of automation in the workflow.
2. **Agent Coordination**: Efficiency of communication between agents.
3. **Data Availability**: Accessibility of required data and resources.

Reducing the time to generate a report ensures faster delivery and higher scalability for JDHP.

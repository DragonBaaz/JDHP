# What is Defining Success Metrics?

Defining success metrics involves identifying measurable criteria to evaluate the performance and effectiveness of the agentic publishing house. These metrics help track progress, identify areas for improvement, and ensure alignment with goals.

## Key Objectives:
1. **Time Efficiency**: Measure the time taken to generate and publish reports.
2. **Quality Assurance**: Evaluate the quality of research reports based on feedback.
3. **Sales Performance**: Track revenue and sales metrics to assess market success.

By defining clear success metrics, JDHP can ensure continuous improvement and achieve its objectives effectively.

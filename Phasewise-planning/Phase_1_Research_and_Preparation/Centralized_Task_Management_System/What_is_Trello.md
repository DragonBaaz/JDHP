# What is Trello?

Trello is a web-based task management tool that uses boards, lists, and cards to organize and prioritize tasks. It is highly visual and user-friendly, making it ideal for managing workflows.

## Key Features:
1. **Boards and Cards**: Organize tasks visually.
2. **Collaboration**: Share boards with team members and assign tasks.
3. **Integrations**: Connect with other tools like Slack and Google Drive.

Trello is a great choice for managing tasks in the JDHP publishing house.

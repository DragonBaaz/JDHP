# What is Notion?

Notion is an all-in-one workspace for note-taking, task management, and collaboration. It provides a flexible platform for organizing and managing information.

## Key Features:
1. **Customizable Pages**: Create pages for tasks, notes, and databases.
2. **Collaboration**: Share and edit pages with team members.
3. **Integrations**: Connect with tools like Slack and Google Calendar.

Notion is a versatile tool for managing tasks and information in the JDHP publishing house.

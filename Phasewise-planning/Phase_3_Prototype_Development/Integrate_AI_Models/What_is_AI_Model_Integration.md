# What is AI Model Integration?

AI model integration refers to the process of embedding AI systems into the JDHP publishing house's workflows. This enables automation and enhances the quality of research reports.

## Key Features:
1. **API Connectivity**: Use APIs to connect AI models with the system.
2. **Customization**: Tailor AI outputs to meet specific requirements.
3. **Scalability**: Handle multiple tasks simultaneously with AI.

AI model integration is essential for automating the research report creation process.

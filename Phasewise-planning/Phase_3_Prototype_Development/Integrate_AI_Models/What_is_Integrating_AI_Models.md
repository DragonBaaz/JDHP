# What is Integrating AI Models?

Integrating AI models involves connecting advanced AI systems to the JDHP publishing house to automate tasks like research report generation. This ensures efficiency and scalability in operations.

## Key Objectives:
1. Use AI models like Claude or OpenAI GPT for text generation.
2. Ensure seamless integration with other agents and workflows.
3. Optimize AI performance for accuracy and relevance.

AI model integration is a cornerstone of the JDHP publishing house's automation strategy.

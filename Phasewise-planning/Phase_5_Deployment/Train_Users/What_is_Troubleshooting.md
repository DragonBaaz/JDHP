# What is Troubleshooting?

Troubleshooting involves identifying and resolving issues that users encounter while operating the JDHP publishing house system. This ensures that the system remains functional and user-friendly.

## Key Features:
1. **Issue Identification**: Detect and diagnose problems.
2. **Resolution**: Implement fixes to resolve issues.
3. **Support**: Provide assistance to users as needed.

Troubleshooting is a critical part of user training and system maintenance.

# What is High Availability?

High availability refers to designing the JDHP publishing house system to minimize downtime and ensure continuous operation. This is achieved through redundancy and fault-tolerant infrastructure.

## Key Features:
1. **Redundancy**: Use multiple servers or resources to prevent single points of failure.
2. **Load Balancing**: Distribute traffic evenly across resources.
3. **Monitoring**: Detect and resolve issues proactively.

High availability is critical for ensuring the reliability of the JDHP publishing house system.
